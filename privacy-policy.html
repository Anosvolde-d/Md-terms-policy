Last updated: February 8, 2026

TL;DR: Mdkey Bot does not store, collect, or share your personal information. We only process data temporarily to provide bot functionality, and all data is deleted when your session ends.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Mdkey Bot ("we," "our," or "the bot"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Discord bot service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Mdkey Bot is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT:

  • Store your personal information in any database
  • Collect or log your Discord messages
  • Track your activity across servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Sell your information to anyone
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing
  • Store conversation history after your session ends

3. Temporary Data Processing

To provide bot functionality, we temporarily process the following information in memory only:

3.1 Session Data

  • User ID: Your Discord user ID to identify your session
  • Server ID: The Discord server where you're using the bot
  • Conversation Context: Your messages during an active setup session
  • Session Duration: Sessions automatically expire after 30 minutes of inactivity

3.2 Operational Logs

  • Action Logs: Temporary logs of bot actions (channel creation, permission changes) for debugging
  • Error Logs: Technical error information to improve bot reliability
  • Retention: All logs are automatically deleted after 7 days

3.3 Rate Limiting Data

  • Request Counts: Number of requests per user to prevent abuse
  • Retention: Automatically cleared after 1 hour

4. Data Storage

All data processed by Mdkey Bot is stored temporarily in memory (RAM) only. We do NOT use:

  • Permanent databases
  • File storage systems
  • Cloud storage services
  • External data warehouses

When the bot restarts, all data is permanently deleted.

5. Third-Party Services

Mdkey Bot uses the following third-party services:

5.1 Discord API

  • We use Discord's official API to provide bot functionality
  • Discord's Privacy Policy applies: https://discord.com/privacy

5.2 OpenRouter AI Service

  • We use OpenRouter to process natural language requests
  • Your messages are sent to OpenRouter's API for AI processing
  • OpenRouter's Privacy Policy applies: https://openrouter.ai/privacy
  • We do not control how OpenRouter processes data

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect temporary data:

  • Access Control: Only authorized users can execute setup commands
  • Rate Limiting: Protection against abuse and spam
  • Secure Communication: All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • No Persistent Storage: Data cannot be accessed after sessions end

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: Since we don't store data, there's nothing to access
  • Right to Deletion: Data is automatically deleted when sessions expire
  • Right to Stop Using: You can stop using the bot at any time
  • Right to Information: Contact us with any privacy questions

8. Children's Privacy

Mdkey Bot does not knowingly collect information from users under 13 years of age. Discord's Terms of Service require users to be at least 13 years old. If you believe a child under 13 is using the bot, please contact us.

9. International Users

Mdkey Bot can be used from anywhere in the world. By using the bot, you consent to the processing of data as described in this Privacy Policy, regardless of your location.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:

  • Investigate the incident immediately
  • Notify affected users if personal data was compromised
  • Take steps to prevent future breaches

Note: Since we don't store personal data permanently, the risk of data breaches is minimal.

12. Your Consent

By using Mdkey Bot, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. If you do not agree, please do not use the bot.
